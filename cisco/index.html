Our Brand Story

As beginnings go, ours was pretty humble.

Husband and wife Len Bosack and Sandy Lerner were employees at Stanford University.

It was 1984 and Sandy wanted to communicate with her husband in another building across campus. They developed the multiprotocol router allowing the first computer network to communicate intelligently with another.

This young couple helped lay the foundation for what makes the Internet possible - the network.

The year was 1984. The internet as we know it didn’t exist. Phones were actually used for calls. And faxing was the most popular way to conduct business.

Over the past thirty years, we’ve built a reputation as a networking powerhouse. But we’re far more than that.

We’re helping everyone from individuals to entire governments reimagine the possibilities created by digitization.

Because at Cisco, we securely connect everything to make anything possible.

We are Cisco Our Creative Expression

“Your brand is what other people say about you when you’re not in the room.” Jeff Bezos CEO of Amazon

This is how we will bring our brand personality to life in all our communications.

Honestly simple Keep it as simple and reduced as possible. Add back just what is needed to get the personality across. Restraint is key.

Quietly bold Be bold without being bombastic. Start with ideas that are strong in and of themselves, then communicate them with restraint.

Thoughtfully optimistic Our work should come across as smart, not naïve. Be optimistic but remember, keep it in check with intelligence and a healthy dose of reality.

Grounded in the future We don’t spend time looking back at what we’ve done. Celebrate what is possible. Break new ground.

Enthusiasm you can feel There should be a sense of energy present in the work we produce. People should be able to feel we love what we do. Because we do.

By making sure that our communications live up to all five of these traits, we’ll be certain everything we do is authentically Cisco. And authentically unlike anything else.
